Fall 2019 InstallFest + Vim Tech Talk!

We will be having an Installfest on Tuesday, September 17 from 6-8PM in Lawson B151! Come by if you wish to get help in installing Linux or to give help to those who need it, or even if you're just curious about Linux!

Coming up after that on Thursday the 19th at 6PM in Recitation 226 we will be having our bi-weekly member meetup, featuring a short Tech Talk on the basics of using the Vim text editor. We'll also have our regular "show-and-share" where you can share something you're interested in, or something you've been working on, in as much or as little detail as you like!

We've got a great semester full of interesting events coming up, so be sure to come by and check us out! You can stay on top of our events with our calendar and subscribing to our mailing list. Hope to see you!
