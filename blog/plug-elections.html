PLUG Elections!

Elections for officers is coming up! There will be a member meeting on Tuesday April 9th in LWSN B151. Any Purdue student that is a member of PLUG can run for an officer position. You can reference our Constitution here

The primary positions open will be President, Vice President, Treasurer, Secretary, Web Master, and Member at Large. There may be other positions open depending on the amount of people who attend! If you have any questions, send me an email!

What: PLUG Elections
When: Tuesday, April 9th at 6-7pm
Where: LWSN B151
